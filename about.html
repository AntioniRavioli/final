About the Creator:

Hi! I'm Anthony Josh Legrama and I have been playing Genshin since the second half of 2021. When I first started playing Genshin, I was lost in how to make a successful team composition and how to build certain characters.

In the beginning, I just gave my characters whichever loot I got from killing enemies and gave them to my characters not knowing what they do to them.

As I kept playing and experimenting with different characters throughout the year, I was able to learn what was best for which character.

Nowadays, I can just simply look at a character's kit and I can judge by myself which weapons and artifacts are good for that character.

So Why Make The Website?

I made this website so that NEW Genshin Impact players will have a better idea on how to build their characters and how they want to organize their team.

Just like I said before, I had a hard time understanding what was great for certain characters. Each character has their own unique style, yet they provide similar values to a team composition.

I want to provide NEW Genshin Impact players the information they need to get a head-start into having a solid account. The time and resin spent on items on this game is valuable as the maxing your resin will take hours.

Learning how to spend your time and resin wisely will make it easier for Genshin Impact players to progress through building your characters, weapons, and artifacts much more smoothly.
